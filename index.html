INDIAN FLAG

    🔔Significance of Indian Flag Colors and Symbols:

    Orange Color: Represents courage and sacrifice.

    White Color: Denotes peace and truth.

    Green Color: Symbolizes faith and chivalry.

    🔔Indian National Flag Meaning

  • Ashoka Chakra: Represents the eternal wheel of law.
  • The flag is a horizontal tricolor of deep saffron, white, and green, with the Ashoka Chakra in navy blue at its center.
  • The design of the flag was adopted on July 22, 1947, just before India gained independence from British rule.
  • The flag is a symbol of national pride and unity for the people of India.
  • It is hoisted on important national occasions such as Independence Day and Republic Day.
